Help & FAQ

What is HectoClash? ➕
HectoClash is a competitive math-based game where players solve puzzles to reach the number 100 using digits and math operations. It's fast-paced and score-based.
How do I play the game? ➕
You get 6 single-digit numbers. Use them exactly once to create an equation that equals 100. Use operations like +, -, ×, ÷. Time and accuracy matter!
How does scoring work? ➕
Correct answer: +5 points. Wrong answer: -3 points. Skipped round: -1 point. Beat your opponent to win and increase your rating!
How is rating updated? ➕
If you win: +100 rating. If you lose: -50 rating. Ratings are stored securely in Firebase and displayed on your profile.
Who can I contact for help? ➕
You can reach out to our support team via the official website or check out the tutorials section for walk-throughs and video guides.
